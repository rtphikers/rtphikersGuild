Our Mission

At RTP Desi Hikers, our mission is to inspire and empower novice hikers to embrace the wonders of hiking while fostering camaraderie and respect for nature. Hiking isn't just about conquering trails; it's a transformative experience that connects you with nature, improves fitness, and introduces you to a welcoming community of outdoor enthusiasts.

Why Choose RTP Desi Hikers

We're dedicated to providing a novice-friendly environment for hikers just starting their journey.Our Mantra:

  • Emphasis on safety and hiking essentials
  • Community and lasting friendships
